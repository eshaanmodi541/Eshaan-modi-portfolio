This is the first post on my engineering blog. I built this site as a place to document my projects, share what I'm learning, and keep a public record of my work.

## What to Expect

Posts here will cover:

- **Electronics** — circuit design, PCB layout, embedded systems
- **Software** — systems programming, web dev, tooling
- **Science** — experiments, analysis, and the occasional deep dive

## The Stack

This site is built with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org), styled with [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com), and uses MDX for content. The design is inspired by circuit board aesthetics — dark backgrounds, amber accents like solder traces, and monospace typography throughout.

> The best way to learn is to build. The second best way is to write about what you built.

More to come.
